Selected work — one proof I ship
Gostly — a system I designed & built end-to-end

A regulated team went from zero agent test coverage to deterministic, production-safe replay — without touching production.

Their AI agents touched sanctions, KYC, and payments APIs — so they couldn't be tested in CI without hitting real vendors and real customer data. I built Gostly: a proxy that captures their real traffic once, redacts it in their own tenant, and replays it as a deterministic, generalizing mock their agents are tested against. No live calls. No prod data in CI. No model in the request path.

Replayed tests can never reach a live vendor endpoint — enforced at the boundary, not by config.
Nothing sensitive leaves the tenant; every recording ships with a PII-free attestation.
No LLM in the hot path — responses are deterministic and repeat byte-for-byte.
